## 1.0.0 - 2026-07-17

### Added

- Four procedural rigid Hat accessory silhouette families.
- Interactive Three.js preview with a generic head proxy and body-scale boundaries.
- GLB, OBJ, PNG, and portable project exports.
- Live triangle, dimension, UV, and exposed-edge checks.
- Normal, Classic, and Slender Hat limits verified against official documentation.
- Local project shelf with strict project-file validation.
- Concept brief, constrained reference prompt, and listing starter.
- Editable upload, advance, pricing, commission, and rebate estimate.
- Eight-step Blender, Studio, AFT, and publishing checklist.
- Responsive interface, publication-hygiene checks, automated tests, and CI.
